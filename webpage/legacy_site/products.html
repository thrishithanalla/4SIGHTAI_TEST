Key Functionalities

IPC Section Analysis: The AI Co-pilot analyzes complaints and suggests relevant IPC sections, ensuring accurate categorization and processing. This automated analysis reduces the time officers spend on legal research and improves the consistency of case classifications.

Case Diary Assistance: It aids in building a detailed case diary by organizing information about accused individuals, defendants, witnesses, and locations. The system automatically structures case information, making it easier to track case progression and maintain comprehensive records.

Deposition Question Generation: The AI Co-pilot generates deposition questions, aiding officers in conducting comprehensive interviews. Based on case details and legal requirements, it suggests relevant questions to ensure thorough information gathering during witness and suspect interviews.

Compilation and Analysis of Depositions: It compiles deposition responses, highlighting inconsistencies for further investigation. The AI analyzes multiple depositions to identify contradictions, patterns, and gaps in statements, helping officers focus their investigative efforts more effectively.

Investigative Support: The AI Co-pilot continuously supports investigative officers with information and analytical insights. It provides real-time access to relevant case precedents, procedural guidelines, and analytical tools throughout the investigation process.

Preparing Legal Briefs: A crucial feature is the AI Co-pilot's ability to assist in preparing legal briefs for the investigative officer. It compiles case information, evidence summaries, and legal references into structured formats that meet court requirements, significantly reducing the time spent on documentation and improving the quality of case presentations.
